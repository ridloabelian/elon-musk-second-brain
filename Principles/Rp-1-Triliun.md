# Target Rp 1 Triliun Q1 2030 (Halal Path)

Target: Rp 1 Triliun = ~$60M USD.
Timeline: Q1 2030 (4 Tahun / 48 Bulan).
Laju Pertumbuhan Diperlukan: ~20% MoM consistently.

## Pilihan Model Bisnis
1. **SaaS Massal**
   - 10,000 customers x $500/month = $5M/month = $60M/year.
2. **SaaS Enterprise**
   - 1,000 enterprise x $5,000/month = $5M/month.
3. **Produk Digital / AI Service**
   - 500,000 buyers x Rp 2 juta = Rp 1T (one-time).
   - 100,000 subscribers x Rp 100k/month = Rp 10B/month = Rp 120B/year.

## Anti-Pattern (Apa yang Haram/Dihindari)
- Bisnis riba, syubhat, gharar, judi, pornografi.
- Markup gila-gilaan tanpa value (scam).
- Meeting gak ada decision.
- Outsourcing core technology (bikin sendiri atau pake AI internal).
